B.Tech in Civil Engineering

The B.Tech in Civil Engineering program at DMI Engineering College equips students with skills in construction management, structural analysis, and design. This program prepares graduates to lead infrastructure development projects.

Key Areas of Study:

  • Structural Engineering
  • Environmental Engineering
  • Construction Management
  • Transportation Engineering
  • Water Resources Engineering

Course Duration:

4 years full-time

Eligibility:

Students must have completed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as core subjects.
